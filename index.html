About Me

I’m a Ventura, California native with a creative spirit and an unwavering passion for forging my own path. Growing up with a world-traveling mother and a free-spirited craftsman father, I was raised to value creativity, resilience, and the beauty of individuality. These influences shaped my eclectic approach to life, leading me to explore everything from art and skateboarding in my youth to building a career rooted in both creativity and technical skill.

Today, I’m a licensed cosmetologist, business owner, and software enthusiast with a strong foundation in management, customer service, and design. My journey includes earning a degree in graphic design, a web development certification, and completing an immersive program in full-stack software development. These experiences have fueled my drive to expand my skills and pursue new opportunities in the tech world.

Though life has brought significant challenges, including personal loss and hardship, I remain committed to growth, resilience, and embracing the future. With my beloved Shorkie by my side, I’m determined to create a fulfilling life where independence, creativity, and success come together.
